Prior to co-founding Custom Courier Solutions, Dan enjoyed a ten-year tenure at CD&L, Inc., which was a $250M national provider of courier services. At CD&L Dan held a variety of operational and administrative roles. He began as the finance manager for the Upstate New York area, which quickly led to managing all activities for the area. Shortly thereafter, Dan became the VP GM for the Northeast Region, which grew to be the largest and most profitable region for CD&L. His last position at CD&L was VP of Systems and Standards with responsibility for creating, defining and implementing processes and procedures throughout the organization.
Prior to joining CD&L, Dan had a long tenure in the financial field primarily in banking. Dan has an undergraduate degree from Hamilton College and an MBA from the Simon School at the University of Rochester with a concentration in corporate finance. Dan resides in the Rochester NY area with his wife and three children.

Prior to co-founding Custom Courier Solutions, Chris was the Manager of Business Development at CD&L and led the Company's sales strategies in the Northeast. Prior to CD&L Chris was the Regional Vice President - East Sales Operations at AirNet, a $150M freight carrier.
Chris has been in various National Sales and Senior Operations roles in the supply chain transportation industry since 1982. Chris is the Immediate Past President of the CLDA - Customized Logistics and Delivery Association, formerly the MCAA, holding a board seat for seven consecutive years. The CLDA is the nation’s largest industry trade association. Chris has a BS degree in Business from SUNY Brockport. Chris resides in Saratoga Springs, New York.
